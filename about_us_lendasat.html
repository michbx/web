Our story

Lendasat was born out of painful lessons from the past. Many of us, and many of you, have been victims of the catastrophic failures and collapses of centralized platforms—Mt. Gox, BlockFi, Celsius, FTX, and countless others. These events exposed a common vulnerability: counterparty risk.

We decided that enough is enough. We never want to be at the mercy of opaque institutions again. So, we created Lendasat — a transparent, trust-minimized solution designed to eliminate counterparty risk from the borrowing experience.

No Rehypothecation. Full Transparency.

With Lendasat, your Bitcoin collateral is never rehypothecated. It stays verifiably on-chain and visible to you at all times. You maintain confidence and control over your funds without relying on any centralized intermediary.

Rethinking Borrowing Economics

We're baffled that in 2025, borrowing against Bitcoin — the most pristine, liquid, and programmable collateral ever invented — still comes with interest rates higher than traditional mortgage rates. This is a mispricing we aim to fix.

Our goal is bold but clear: Make borrowing against Bitcoin cheaper than a mortgage. Bitcoin is better collateral than real estate — it is more liquid, easier to transfer, and harder to fake. It's time for borrowing costs to reflect that.
